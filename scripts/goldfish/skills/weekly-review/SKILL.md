# Weekly Review Skill

## Purpose

Turn the last seven days of AI intelligence into a focused weekly review and action plan.

## When To Use

Use this skill for weekly AI trend reports, review sessions, prioritization, and deciding what should become durable knowledge.

## Review Flow

1. Read the last seven daily reports.
2. Extract repeated trends and outliers.
3. Identify the top ten important items.
4. Separate people opinions, product launches, papers, open-source projects, and business opportunities.
5. Recommend permanent notes, prompts, business ideas, and project ideas.
6. Pick next week's watch list.

## Output Sections

- This week's top 10 AI signals
- People-watch highlights
- Agent / RAG / AI Coding trend
- Open-source projects
- Papers worth reading
- Business opportunities
- What to ignore
- Next week's watch list
- Suggested permanent notes
- Suggested business ideas
