# Business Idea Skill

## Purpose

Extract realistic business opportunities from AI intelligence.

## When To Use

Use this skill when a news item, product launch, paper, open-source project, or founder comment may become a paid product, service, content asset, workflow, or micro-SaaS.

## Extraction Frame

For each candidate business idea, identify:

1. Target user
2. Pain point
3. Product shape
4. MVP
5. Pricing model
6. Distribution channel
7. Validation method
8. Risks
9. Why now
10. Suggested Obsidian location

## Scoring Hints

Raise priority when:

- The user can build a prototype quickly.
- The pain point appears in repeated sources.
- The idea connects to Agent, RAG, AI Coding, MCP, or knowledge workflows.
- The buyer is clear.
- The workflow saves time or makes money.

Lower priority when:

- The idea depends on private data.
- The buyer is vague.
- The implementation is mostly commodity.
- The evidence is only hype.
