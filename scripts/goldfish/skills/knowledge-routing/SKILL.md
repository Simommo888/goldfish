# Knowledge Routing Skill

## Purpose

Decide how retrieved information should become durable knowledge assets in the Obsidian vault.

## When To Use

Use this skill after research, daily reports, weekly reviews, or any answer that contains reusable knowledge.

## Routing Rules

1. Long-term concepts go to permanent notes.
2. Product or monetization opportunities go to business ideas.
3. Repeatable instructions go to prompts.
4. Buildable concepts go to project ideas.
5. Temporary or uncertain findings stay in Inbox or Reports.
6. Keep each draft focused on one idea.
7. Preserve source links.

## Destination Map

- Permanent note: `05_Permanent-Notes/AI-Trends`
- Business idea: `11_Business-Ideas/AI-News-Inspirations`
- Prompt: `09_Prompts/AI-News`
- Project idea: `02_Projects/AI-News-Ideas`
- Research report: `04_Resources/AI-News/Reports`
- Raw or uncertain item: `00_Inbox`

## Output Shape

Return:

- `item`
- `destination`
- `draft_type`
- `reason`
- `title`
- `core_points`
- `source_links`
- `next_action`
